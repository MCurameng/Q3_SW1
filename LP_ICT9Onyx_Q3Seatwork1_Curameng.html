Yamashita, Daiki

Daiki Yamashita (山下 大輝, Yamashita Daiki, born September 7, 1989) is a Japanese voice actor affiliated with Arts Vision. He is best known for voicing the character Izuku Midoriya in the popular shonen anime My Hero Academia. He won the Best Male Newcomer award at the 8th Seiyu Awards.

Hanazawa, Kana

Kana Hanazawa (花澤 香菜, Hanazawa Kana, born February 25, 1989) is a Japanese actress, voice actress and singer. A prolific voice performer in anime, she has amassed several film and television credits since her debut in 2003.

Mizuki, Nana

Mizuki was trained as an enka singer, releasing one single under her birth name in 1993 and made her debut as a voice actress in 1996.

Endou, Aya

Aya Endō (遠藤 綾, Endō Aya, born February 17, 1980) is a Japanese voice actress and narrator from Yamagata Prefecture who is currently affiliated with Office PAC. After Yūko Mizutani's death, she became the official Japanese dub voice of Minnie Mouse, and has taken over many of Mizutani's other roles.

Shimada, Bin

Bin Shimada (Japanese: 島田 敏, romanized: Shimada Bin; born November 20, 1954) is a Japanese actor, voice actor and narrator affiliated with the talent management firm Aoni Production.
